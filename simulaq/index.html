The convergence of physics & AI

SimulaQ leverages high-performance C++ and GPU-accelerated computing to solve the Gross-Pitaevskii equation. By integrating learned neural operators, we cut computational overhead in predicting vortex lattice evolution by orders of magnitude.

  • Neural solvers. FDTD and spectral methods optimized via AI surrogate models.
  • Quantum insights. Real-time turbulence and entropy analysis on AFNO-class operators.
  • Scale. Designed for NVIDIA A100 / H100 clusters with LLM-generated CUDA kernels.
